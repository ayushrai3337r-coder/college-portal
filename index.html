Exam Pattern

Theory papers follow Maharashtra University CBCS / NEP 2020 pattern:

  • Q1 MCQs — 20 marks (2 marks each)
  • Q2 Short notes — 16 marks (4 × 4)
  • Q3–Q5 Long answers — 24 marks (3 × 8, attempt 3 of 5)
  • Passing Min 36% in theory + 36% in practical separately
  • Duration 3 hours per paper
  • Practical Internal 10 + Journal 5 + Viva 5 = 20
